About

Mandy Prouting

Mandy is a New Zealand healthcare professional with a background in radiation therapy and undergraduate psychology. Her clinical experience has shaped a deep interest in palliative care, existential wellbeing, grief, communication, and the human experience of illness and healing.

She is currently undertaking further education in psychedelic-assisted therapy and is particularly interested in ethically grounded, relational, and evidence-informed approaches to care.

This site is not a clinical service. It is a developing space for professional identity, thoughtful writing, future collaboration, and careful conversation.
