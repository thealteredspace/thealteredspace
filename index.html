About

Guided By Mandy is a reflective professional space created by Mandy Prouting — a radiation therapist with undergraduate training in psychology and a growing interest in psychedelic-assisted therapy, palliative care, integration, grief, and compassionate approaches to meaning-making.

This is not a clinical psychedelic service. It is a space for thoughtful reflection, emerging ideas, interdisciplinary dialogue, and grounded conversation around the human experience.

My interests sit at the intersection of medicine, psychology, embodiment, consciousness, mortality, and the ways people search for meaning through suffering, healing, connection, and change.
