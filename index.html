About

Guided By Mandy is a personal and evolving project created by Mandy Prouting — a radiation therapist with undergraduate training in psychology and a growing interest in psychedelic-assisted therapy, palliative care, grief, and integration.

This space exists at the intersection of medicine, mortality, consciousness, and meaning-making.

Rather than offering certainty, it explores thoughtful reflection, ethical curiosity, emotionally grounded discussion, and the complexities of being human.
